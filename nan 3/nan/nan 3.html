Discover the Cozy Charm of Linda Cloth House

Step into the warm embrace of Linda Cloth House, nestled in the heart of Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. Here, we've crafted an oasis of calm where fabric and conversation intertwine, inviting you to slow down and savor the moment.. Our goal is to provide an authentic and immersive cloth shopping experience, where the textures of fine fabrics mingle with the gentle hum of friendly chatter. Sink into our plush, cozy couches and let the world fade away as you indulge in the perfect bolt of cloth, selected with meticulous care and attention to detail. At Linda Cloth House, we believe that the simple act of discovering beautiful textiles can be an art form, a moment of respite in the bustling city. Come and experience the warmth, the charm, and the genuine hospitality that make our shop a beloved local treasure.

Why Us?

To become stylish

Our expertly curated selection of festive and resort-style clothing will ensure you look and feel your best for any holiday or vacation celebration. Our in-house stylists are here to help you put together the perfect outfit to make your special occasion truly memorable.
